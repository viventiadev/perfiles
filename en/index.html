Innovation Profile Questionnaire

This questionnaire is designed to describe the individual method that each person has when solving problems and facing innovative situations.

Please give a high score to those words that best define the way you solve your problems and score low those terms that to a lesser degree characterize your problem-solving style.

If you think that none of them fits correctly or you do not consider that any term defines it correctly, use your own intuition to give the answer. You may find it difficult to choose the words that best describe your own problem-solving style as there are no right or wrong answers. Different characteristics are equally good. The purpose of this questionnaire is to help describe how you solve your problems and not to evaluate your ability to solve them. Therefore, there are no wrong answers.

Directions: This page will display 18 groups of 4 words in horizontal line. Rank each of the 18 sets of words from 4 to 1.

On each horizontal line, assign 4 points to the concept that best characterizes your problem-solving style, 3 points to the next term that best does it, 2 points to the next, and 1 point to the concept that you think least characterizes your style.
You must rate the four concepts in each row with different numbers.
The total for each row must be 10.
